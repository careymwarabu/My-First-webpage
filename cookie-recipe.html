Best Chocolate Chip Cookies

This recipe is from my dad and it's a favorite among friends and family. The secret ingredient is the coconut! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

History of cookies

Most people don't know the complicated past of the cookie.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325 degrees.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
